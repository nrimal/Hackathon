About Us

We are a group of four college students, who desired to help the community. We were confronted with a challenge, presented by JPMorgan Chase & Co., to build a web or mobile based app that provides accessability information to individuals with disabilities. This inspired us to create a free web-based app that empowers those with disabilities by providing them the tools to view the accesability information of restaurants, theaters, and other venues of their choice.

Our Mission

Enhance

To enable those with disabilities by giving them the tool to find venues that are accomodating and safe.

Advance

Set a higher standard of handicap accessability for businesses and venues around the country..

Transform

To heighten awareness of the general need for greater handicap accomodation around the country.
